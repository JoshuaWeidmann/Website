I am a Masters student in Politics and Public Administration at the University of Konstanz. I hold an undergraduate degree in Political Science and Economics from the University of Mannheim and an MSc in Political Sociology from the London School of Economics and Political Science.. In addition, I work as a Research Assistant for Michael M. Bechtel (University of Cologne) and Mathias Poertner (LSE).

My research interests lie in Historical Political Economy, Political Behaviour, Survey Methodology, and Causal Inference.
